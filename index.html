About Luna Energy

We’re on a mission to make EV charging as simple and safe as charging your phone. Our approach brings together rugged hardware, human‑centered design, and cloud intelligence.

Safety first

Monsoon‑ready, SELV‑compliant designs and fault detection baked into the hardware for Indian conditions.

Smart by default

IoT telemetry, analytics, and remote management — enabling dynamic pricing, load balancing, and alerts.

Affordable & scalable

Modular components, tileable pavement chargers, and misalignment‑tolerant receivers for quick deployment.
